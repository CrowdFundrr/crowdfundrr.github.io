A Better Way To Crowd Fund

CrowdFundrr is a free to use, totally open-source crowd funding platform. We believe the internet should be a place where creativity can shine, which is why we are a no-fee, open-sourced crowd funding platform.

Built on Ethereum

CrowdFundrr is entirely built on Ethereum, a decentralized open-source ecosystem that enables a more free and secure internet.

Safe & Secure

CrowdFundrr uses robust and thoroughly tested open-source parts such as bootstrap, Ethereum, and JQuery.
