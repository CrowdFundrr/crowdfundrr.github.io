About

WeiFund is a decentralized, non-profit, open-source crowdfunding platform that can be extended to create and connect with any token or share system you can imagine.

What if crowdfunding contributions could be turned into contractually backed equity shares or custom tokens? Where support for a company, project or idea can be converted into real digital assets. WeiFund makes possible, all this and more. A free, open-source and secure platform to crowdfund your projects within the Ethereum ecosystem.

Crowdfunding

Start, discover, operate and contribute to crowdfunding campaigns.

Token & Share Systems

Turn campaign contributions into real digital equity shares, tokens or what ever you can imagine.

Our Philosophy

Make crowdfunding as free, open, secure and extendable as possible.
